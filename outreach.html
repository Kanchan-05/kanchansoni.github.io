Outreach & Public Engagement

I actively engage in popularizing gravitational-wave astrophysics through talks, mentoring, workshops, and science communication.

Selected Activities

  1. Mentor, GW Open Science Data Workshop (2022)
    Participated as a mentor, helping students and early-career researchers explore gravitational-wave data and tools.
  2. Member, LIGO-India Education and Public Outreach Team (2020–2022)
    Contributed to organizing talks, student interactions, and outreach programs across India.
  3. Introductory Talk: “Detection of Gravitational Waves” at Naxxatra Science, India
    Aimed at high school and undergraduate students to build awareness of the science behind gravitational waves.
  4. Science Summary: A Symphony of Giant Monsters
    Wrote a science explainer on the search for intermediate-mass black hole binaries during LIGO-Virgo's third observing run.
  5. Alumni Talk: “Listening to Cosmic Whispers”
    Delivered at IISER Thiruvananthapuram to inspire current students with recent advancements in gravitational-wave astronomy.
